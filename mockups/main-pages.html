How it started

Started for one campsite. Designed for the rest.

Pakkia began as a custom system for a single lakeside site tired of re-typing totals every month. It worked — so we rebuilt it as a platform any Finnish campsite can switch on in an afternoon.

We kept it deliberately narrow. Pakkia is not a booking engine or a payment processor. It does one regulated job well, stays out of the way the rest of the time, and keeps your data inside the EU.

How it works

Four steps from set-up to a filed report.

The hard part — totals, formatting, traceability — happens on its own. Your team only ever does step two.

Step 01

Set up your pitches

Add your pitch numbers and areas once. An admin assigns each holder to a pitch and defines the season dates.

~ one afternoon
Step 02

Log the nights

Staff or pitch holders open the calendar and enter the number of persons per night. One tap, one number — on any phone.
